Privacy Policy for TriagePaw

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

TriagePaw ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered pet triage application.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in via Google or Apple, we collect your email address and name via Firebase Authentication.
  • Device Identifiers (Anti-Fraud): To prevent abuse of our "Welcome Credit" system, we collect a unique hardware identifier (Device ID) from your mobile device. This is used solely to verify if a device has already claimed a free trial.
  • Triage Data: We process the pet health information you provide to generate AI triage suggestions. This data is stored securely in our Supabase database.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following secure partners to provide our services:

  • Supabase: For secure database storage and data management.
  • Firebase (Google): For secure user authentication and deep linking.
  • Resend: To send critical support and transactional emails.

4. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You have the right to request a copy of your data or its deletion at any time by contacting us.
